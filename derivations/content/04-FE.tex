\section{Free energy}

The local free energy can be calculated (by a Taylor expansion) as 
\begin{equation}
    \begin{split}
        U
        &= -\mathrm{E}_{q(x)q(s)q(n)} \left[\ln p(x\mid s, n)\right] \\
        &\approx \ln(\pi) + \ln(e^{m_s} + e^{m_n}) +\frac{v_x + |m_x|^2}{e^{m_s} + e^{m_n}}
    \end{split}
\end{equation}
